OUR HISTORY

Gigatec LLC is women-owned small business consulting firm dedicated to building business partnerships and achieving strategic customer focused outcomes for the United States Department of Defense and Intelligence Community. Our Team has a passion for providing solutions that advance our nation’s intelligence operations and protect National Security Systems from adversaries and emerging threats. We bring 20+ years of experience and a proven track record supporting mission-critical areas through Systems Engineering and Cloud Architectures, Data Science and Analytics, Computer Network Operations, and Cybersecurity. Gigatec LLC operates with the goal of enabling mission success through subject matter expertise, bridging technical and operations experts to achieve engineering solutions while leveraging cloud technologies and data-driven decision-making capabilities.
